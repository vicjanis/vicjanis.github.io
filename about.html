Who Am I?

Vic!

While at the University at Buffalo I studied graphic design and minored in architecture. These two studies of design with the addition of starting in the engineering program strongly shaped my concept of art.

My experiences in school included the Adobe Suite with most of my time in Illustrator, Photoshop and Indesign. In the gallery page I have examples of business cards and small keep sake cards, 3D modeling done in Rhino7, large scale painting, and this website is an example of my web design abilities.

As a professional, I find heavy interest in print making, typography, page layout design, and formating for production. In the more fluid realm of being an artist, I explore wood and metal sculpture, installation, and larger relief prints. I am highly influenced by nature, the cityscape, and advertisements. Each of these subjects is always morphing and creating more to learn about and discover.

Please reach out with any questions or if you're looking to collaborate!
